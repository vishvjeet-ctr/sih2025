The Problem

Understanding the challenges in small city transportation

Unpredictable Schedules

Buses arrive without warning, leaving commuters waiting indefinitely at stops with no real-time information.

Overcrowding Issues

Peak hours see buses packed beyond capacity, while off-peak times have empty buses running inefficiently.

No Real-Time Tracking

Commuters have no way to know where their bus is, when it will arrive, or if it's running on schedule.

Increased Private Vehicle Use

Unreliable public transport forces people to use private vehicles, increasing traffic and pollution.

Privacy & Security

Your data privacy and security are our top priorities

Data Collection

We only collect location data when you explicitly opt-in for real-time tracking. All data is anonymized and used solely for improving transport services.

Data Security

All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols. We never share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your data at any time. You can also opt-out of location tracking and data collection through the app settings.
